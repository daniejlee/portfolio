About

Hi, my name is Daniel, and I'm a full stack developer with an obsession for coding and a passion for problem solving.

I graduated from UCI with a Bachelor's degree in Informatics, and a specialization in Human Computer Interaction. Through my coursework, I learned about the various aspects of the software development process, such as requirements gathering, UI/UX design, prototyping, testing, and working in an Agile environment. Almost every project done during the curriculum was done in teams and in doing so, I have truly come to understand the importance of effective communication and collaboration.

From there, I decided to commit to a 14 week Full-Immersion, Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp at LearningFuze, where I spent 10 to 12+ hours coding every day. It was here that I really fell in love with writing code and using my skills to reach a goal in an elegant and effective way.
